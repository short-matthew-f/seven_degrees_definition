Welcome to Seven Degrees of Definition

The goal of this game is to make a chain of words using definitions provided by Merriam-Webster.

One player chooses two words, a first word and a last word.

The other player is given with the definition provided by the first word. They can click any word in the definition to fetch another definition, and so on until they click the last word.
